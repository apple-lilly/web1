Lover

Lover is the seventh studio album by Taylor Swift. As the executive producer, Swift enlisted producers Jack Antonoff, Louis Bell, Frank Dukes and Joel Little for the album. Described by Swift as "a love letter to love itself", Lover celebrates the ups and downs of love, incorporating lighter and brighter tones, departing from the dark sounds of its predecessor, Reputation(2017). i can say that everyone would agree that it is the peak of her music career. her incredible and also improved songwriting and producing skill is contained in this album.
